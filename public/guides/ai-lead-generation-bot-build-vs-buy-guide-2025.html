AI Lead Generation Bot: Build vs Buy Guide for Startups

Quick Answer: Buy off-the-shelf lead gen bots for simple email/form capture ($50-500/month). Build custom AI voice/chat agents when you need qualification logic, CRM integration, or voice conversations ($7k-35k).

Published October 12, 2025

The Lead Gen Bot Spectrum

Level What It Does Cost Setup Time
Level 1: Form Capture Pop-up, email capture $50-500/month Same day
Level 2: Chatbot Qualification questions, scoring $300-2k/month 1-3 days
Level 3: AI Chat Agent Natural conversation, CRM integration $5k-8k pilot | $20k-35k prod 6-8 days
Level 4: Voice Agent Outbound/inbound calls, qualification $7k-9k pilot | $28k-40k prod 6-10 days

Build vs Buy Decision Framework

Buy Off-the-Shelf If:

  • Simple use case (email capture, basic qualification)
  • Budget under $5k
  • Need live today
  • Standard qualification questions
  • Low volume (<1,000 leads/month)
  • Text chat is sufficient

Platforms to Consider: Drift, Qualified, Intercom, HubSpot Chat

Build Custom If:

  • Complex qualification logic (industry-specific)
  • Budget $5k+
  • Voice conversations needed
  • Deep CRM integration
  • High volume (1,000+ leads/month)
  • Unique workflows
  • Competitive advantage (your process is your edge)

Cost Comparison: Real Examples

Example 1: SaaS Startup (Early Stage)

Scenario: 200 website visitors/day, simple qualification

Drift: $1,200/month → Year 1: ~$14,500

Custom AI Chat: $25k production → Year 1: ~$31k

Winner: Drift (volume doesn't justify custom)

Example 2: Real Estate Brokerage

Scenario: 500 leads/month, need voice calls to qualify

Drift: Can't do voice

Custom Voice Agent: $32k → Year 1: ~$42k

Winner: Custom (only option for voice)

ROI: Replaced 2 FTE phone reps ($80k/year), payback in 6 months

Example 3: B2B Enterprise Software

Scenario: High-ticket ($100k+ ACV), complex qualification

Qualified: $2,400/month → Year 1: ~$29k

Custom: $45k → Year 1: ~$53k

Winner: Depends on volume

ROI Model: If custom increases qualification by 10% → 200 extra qualified leads/year → 40 extra deals → $4M revenue → 75x ROI

Platform Options: Off-the-Shelf

Drift

  • Cost: $2,500/month (Pro)
  • Best for: B2B SaaS, inbound chat
  • Pros: Fast setup, ABM features, Salesforce integration
  • Cons: No voice, expensive at scale

Qualified

  • Cost: $3,500/month
  • Best for: Enterprise, Salesforce-heavy
  • Pros: Sales-first, signals, routing intelligence
  • Cons: Expensive, Salesforce-centric, no voice

Intercom

  • Cost: $499/month
  • Best for: Support + sales hybrid
  • Pros: Affordable, good CRM integrations
  • Cons: More support-focused, no voice

Custom Build: What You Can Do

Voice Agent (Outbound)

Use Case: Call leads to qualify, book meetings

How It Works:

  1. Lead comes in (web form, ad, list)
  2. AI calls within 5 minutes
  3. Asks 7-10 qualification questions
  4. Handles objections, books if qualified
  5. Logs to CRM, notifies sales rep

Cost: $7k pilot | $30k-35k production

ROI: Replaces 1-2 FTEs, 24/7 availability, 60-80% qualified by AI

Voice Agent (Inbound)

Use Case: Answer incoming calls, qualify, route

Cost: $6k-7k pilot | $28k-32k production

ROI: After-hours coverage, reduced phone staff, 100% answer rate

AI Chat Agent (Website)

Use Case: Website chat, intelligent qualification

Cost: $5k-7k pilot | $22k-28k production

ROI: Better qualification, higher engagement, 24/7 availability

ROI Calculation Framework

Formula: Annual Value = (Leads Qualified × Close Rate × ACV) - Total Cost

Example: B2B SaaS

Inputs:

  • Leads/month: 500
  • Bot qualifies: 70% (350/month vs human 40% = 200/month)
  • Extra qualified: 150/month = 1,800/year
  • Close rate: 15%
  • Deals from bot: 270/year
  • ACV: $25k

Revenue from bot: $6.75M/year

Costs: $50k (Year 1)

ROI: 134x

Voice vs Chat for Lead Gen

Choose Voice If:

  • High ACV (>$25k)
  • Personal touch matters
  • Complex sale (B2B, multiple stakeholders)
  • Older demographic (prefer phone)
  • Outbound required

Choose Chat If:

  • Lower ACV (<$10k)
  • Younger demographic (prefer text)
  • Simple qualification
  • Inbound only (website visitors)
  • Budget-conscious

Key Takeaways

  • Buy platforms for simple use cases (<$5k budget, basic qualification)
  • Build custom for voice, complex logic, high volume
  • Voice agents: ROI 3-6 months (replace FTEs)
  • Chat agents: ROI 6-12 months (incremental revenue)
  • High ACV justifies voice investment
  • Pilot first ($6k-9k) before production commitment
