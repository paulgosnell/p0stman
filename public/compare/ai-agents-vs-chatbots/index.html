Which Should You Choose?

Honest recommendations for each option

When to Use Traditional Chatbot

Use traditional chatbots for simple FAQ scenarios, providing information only, or when you just need basic customer service automation without system integration.

When to Use AI Agent

Use AI agents when you need task completion (booking, updating records), system integration (CRM, calendar, payment), or intelligent decision-making beyond simple Q&A.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my chatbot to an AI agent?

Depends on the platform. If your chatbot is rule-based or template-driven, you'll likely need to rebuild it with proper AI capabilities and system integrations. We can assess your current bot and recommend the best path.

Do I even need an AI agent, or is a chatbot enough?

Ask: do you want it to DO things or just TELL things? If you need appointment booking, CRM updates, or workflow automation - you need an agent. For FAQs and basic support info - a chatbot works.

Are AI agents harder to maintain?

Not necessarily. Good AI agents are easier to update because they use natural language understanding rather than brittle rule trees. Change behavior by adjusting prompts, not rewiring logic trees.
