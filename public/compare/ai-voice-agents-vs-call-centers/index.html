Which Should You Choose?

Honest recommendations for each option

When to Use Call Center (Human Agents)

Use human call centers for complex problem-solving, emotionally sensitive situations (healthcare, crisis), sales requiring persuasion, or when empathy is critical.

When to Use AI Voice Agents

Use AI voice agents for appointment scheduling, FAQs, order status, payment processing, lead qualification, or any repetitive, high-volume tasks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will AI voice agents replace all call center jobs?

No. They'll replace repetitive tasks (appointment booking, FAQs). Human agents shift to complex problem-solving and emotionally sensitive calls. Smart companies use AI to augment, not eliminate, their team.

What's the quality difference?

For simple tasks: AI is better (faster, more consistent, no hold times). For complex problems: humans are better (judgment, creativity, empathy). Use each for what they're best at.

How do customers react to AI agents?

75%+ don't notice or don't care - if the AI solves their problem quickly. 20% prefer AI (no small talk, faster). 5% insist on humans - give them the option to escalate anytime.
