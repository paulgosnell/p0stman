Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI lead scoring work?

AI analyzes lead attributes (company size, industry, role) and behavior (pages visited, content downloaded, emails opened) to predict likelihood to buy. Scores update in real-time as leads engage.

What data can be enriched automatically?

Company size, industry, revenue, technology stack, funding status, LinkedIn profiles, social presence, news mentions, and more. Most leads can be fully enriched within seconds of form submission.
