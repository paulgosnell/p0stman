12-Week Intensive Curriculum

Our bootcamp is structured to take you from product management novice to job-ready professional in just 12 weeks.

Weeks 1-3: Product Management Foundations

  • Product Mindset: Understanding the PM role and responsibilities
  • Business Strategy: How products fit into company strategy and goals
  • User-Centric Thinking: Putting users at the center of product decisions
  • Agile Methodology: Scrum, Kanban, and modern development practices
  • Product Lifecycle: From idea to launch to growth and sunset

Weeks 4-6: Research & Discovery

  • User Research: Interviews, surveys, and usability testing techniques
  • Market Analysis: Competitive analysis and market sizing
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Using analytics to guide product strategy
  • Problem Definition: Crafting clear problem statements and hypotheses
  • Opportunity Identification: Finding product opportunities in the market

Weeks 7-9: Strategy & Planning

  • Product Vision: Creating compelling product visions and roadmaps
  • Prioritization Frameworks: RICE, Kano, MoSCoW, and custom frameworks
  • Requirements Gathering: Working with stakeholders to define requirements
  • Risk Management: Identifying and mitigating product risks
  • Go-to-Market Strategy: Planning successful product launches

Weeks 10-12: Execution & Growth

  • Cross-Functional Leadership: Working effectively with engineering, design, and marketing
  • Metrics & Analytics: Defining and tracking product success metrics
  • Growth Hacking: Driving user acquisition and retention
  • Product Marketing: Positioning and messaging for product success
  • Career Development: Building your personal brand and network

Real-World Projects You'll Lead

Hands-on experience is the best teacher. You'll lead real product initiatives from conception to launch.

Project 1: User Research & Problem Validation

Conduct comprehensive user research to validate a real problem:

  • User Interviews: Conduct 20+ user interviews to understand pain points
  • Competitive Analysis: Analyze 10+ competitors and identify gaps
  • Problem Statement: Craft a clear, actionable problem statement
  • Validation Testing: Test assumptions with landing pages and surveys
  • Stakeholder Presentation: Present findings to executive stakeholders

Skills Learned: User research, competitive analysis, stakeholder communication | Deliverable: Research report and presentation

Project 2: Product Strategy & Roadmap

Build a complete product strategy for a startup idea:

  • Market Opportunity: Size the market and identify target segments
  • Product Vision: Create a compelling vision and mission statement
  • User Personas: Develop detailed user personas and use cases
  • Feature Prioritization: Use frameworks to prioritize features
  • Roadmap Creation: Build a 12-month product roadmap

Skills Learned: Strategic planning, prioritization, roadmap creation | Deliverable: Product strategy document and roadmap

Project 3: Product Launch & Growth

Launch a product and drive initial user acquisition:

  • Go-to-Market Plan: Develop positioning, messaging, and launch strategy
  • Prototype Development: Work with designers to create a clickable prototype
  • Marketing Campaign: Plan and execute a product launch campaign
  • User Acquisition: Drive initial users through various channels
  • Metrics Dashboard: Set up tracking for key product metrics

Skills Learned: Product launch, growth marketing, metrics tracking | Deliverable: Launched product with user acquisition results
