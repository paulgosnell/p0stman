Consumer health decisions are paralysed by noise and fragmentation.

Limited Literacy

Fragmented Journey

Equity Gap

Solution

No house brands. No pay-to-rank. Transparent disclosures separating evidence, fit, and commercial terms.

05
Product Storyboard

Claim → Evidence Summary → Vetted Options → Action.

We choreograph a single flow so users can clarify needs, understand evidence, and act with confidence.

  • Standardised listings with evidence, fit, and commercial disclosures separated for trust.
  • Proprietary evidence engine triages research and labels strength with human-in-the-loop governance.
  • Topic hubs spanning tests, supplements, programmes, and clinics to guide exploration.
  • Access and routing to vetted merchants/clinics; selective on-site checkout when unit economics prove out.
  • Community design for Q&A, testimonials, and early expert insights.
