Our Independence Promise

We make money when you make good decisions, not when brands pay us to promote their products.

No Pay-to-List

Brands can't buy their way onto our marketplace. Every product earns its spot through our testing process, not their marketing budget.

No House Brands

We don't make or sell our own products. This eliminates the temptation to promote inferior products just because they're ours.

Transparent Commissions

Yes, we earn small commissions on some purchases. But our ratings are set before any business relationships, never after.

Human Review

AI helps us process research faster, but humans make every final decision about what gets listed and how it's rated.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about how we work

Are you doctors?

No, we're not a medical clinic. We provide evidence summaries and vetted consumer options, not prescriptions or medical advice. Always consult healthcare professionals for serious health concerns.

How do you stay neutral?

Clear separation between evidence scoring and business model. Products are tested and rated before any commercial relationships begin. We also have no financial stake in any specific brands.

Can I trust the ratings?

Every score comes with detailed reasoning and version tracking. Community feedback enriches our understanding, but evidence always comes first. We also publish when and why ratings change.

What if I disagree with a rating?

We welcome feedback! If you have new research or different experiences, share them with us. Ratings are updated when new evidence emerges, and we're transparent about changes.

How often do ratings change?

We review ratings quarterly and update them when significant new research emerges. All changes are documented with reasons, so you can track how our understanding evolves.

Do you test everything personally?

Our team and community members test many products, but we also rely heavily on published research and third-party lab testing. Personal experience is valuable but never overrides scientific evidence.
