What is Menopause Support?

Menopause is a natural biological process marking the end of reproductive years. While hormone replacement therapy remains the gold standard for severe symptoms, many women seek natural alternatives. Evidence varies significantly across different supplements and approaches.

May help manage hot flashes and night sweats
Supports bone health during hormonal changes
May improve mood and sleep quality

Who Should Consider These Supplements?

Mild Symptoms

Hot flashes, mood changes, sleep issues

Hormone-Sensitive Conditions

Breast cancer history requires medical guidance

Severe Symptoms

Consider medical hormone therapy first

When to Consult a Healthcare Provider

  • If you have a history of breast, ovarian, or uterine cancer
  • Before starting any herbal supplements if you're on medications
  • If symptoms significantly impact your quality of life
  • If you experience unusual bleeding patterns
  • Before combining multiple menopause supplements
  • If considering hormone replacement therapy alternatives
