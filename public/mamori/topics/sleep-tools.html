What are Sleep Tools?

Sleep tools encompass devices and aids designed to optimize sleep quality, duration, and tracking. This includes wearable trackers, environmental controls, and comfort aids. While the field is evolving, some tools show promising evidence for improving sleep outcomes.

When to Consult a Healthcare Provider

  • If you have chronic insomnia lasting more than 3 weeks
  • Experience loud snoring or breathing interruptions during sleep
  • Have excessive daytime sleepiness despite adequate sleep time
  • Experience unusual behaviors during sleep (sleepwalking, night terrors)
  • If sleep tools don't improve sleep quality after 4-6 weeks of use
