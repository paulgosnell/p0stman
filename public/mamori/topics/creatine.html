What is Creatine?

Creatine is a naturally occurring compound found in muscle cells that helps produce energy during high-intensity, short-duration exercise. It's one of the most extensively researched supplements in sports nutrition, with consistent evidence showing benefits for strength, power, and muscle mass.

Increases muscle phosphocreatine stores
Enhances ATP regeneration during exercise
Improves performance in repeated high-intensity bouts

Who Should Consider Creatine?

Strength Athletes

Weightlifters, powerlifters, sprinters

Team Sport Athletes

Soccer, basketball, hockey players

Endurance Athletes

Limited benefits for pure endurance

When to Consult a Healthcare Provider

  • If you have kidney disease or reduced kidney function
  • Before use if you're taking medications that affect kidney function
  • If you experience unusual muscle cramping or digestive issues
  • If you're pregnant, breastfeeding, or under 18 years old
  • If you have any concerns about supplementation
