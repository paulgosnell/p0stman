Frequently Asked Questions

How much does construction operations automation cost?

A custom construction operations platform typically costs $15,000-50,000 to build, depending on the number of integrations needed. This replaces $2,000-5,000/month in disconnected SaaS tools and saves 20-30 admin hours per week.

Can I connect Procore to my accounting software?

Yes. Custom integrations can sync Procore with QuickBooks, Xero, or Sage in real-time. This eliminates double-entry, reduces billing errors, and gives you live job costing instead of month-end surprises.

How long does it take to automate construction operations?

Most construction automation projects take 6-12 weeks from discovery to deployment. The first integrations (like Procore to QuickBooks sync) can be live within 2-3 weeks.
