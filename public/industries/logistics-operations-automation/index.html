Frequently Asked Questions

How can logistics companies automate without replacing their TMS?

Custom integrations can connect your existing TMS to accounting, customer portals, and driver apps. You get automation without the risk and cost of switching core systems.

Can AI help with load matching and pricing?

Yes. AI can analyze market rates, driver availability, and historical data to suggest optimal pricing. It can also automatically match loads with the right drivers based on location, certifications, and preferences.

What does logistics automation typically cost?

Basic integrations (TMS to accounting, automated POD capture) cost $10,000-25,000. Full digital transformation with customer portals and AI-powered dispatch costs $30,000-75,000.
